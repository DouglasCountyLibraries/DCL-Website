Kid-friendly Fun:

Plants & Pollinators

Watch imaginations soar as children learn about the plant-pollinator system through hands-on playtime in the apple play house, featuring a specimen table for insect observation. Busy little ones will have fun exploring the world of bees and playing beekeeper in our realistic beekeeping station, complete with materials for role play and dress up. Crawl through the caterpillar, spin the blossoms, and much more while learning about this fascinating ecosystem.
Sponsored by Tagawa Gardens
