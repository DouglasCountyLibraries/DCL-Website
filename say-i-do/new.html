Say I Do at DCL

Weddings, Rehearsal Dinners & Showers

The premium event spaces in our beautiful libraries are the perfect setting for celebrating your love story.

Whether it's an intimate, romantic affair, an elegant soiree, a rehearsal dinner, or festive celebration, our thoughtful event coordinators can help bring your vision to life.

Receive full event planning services with our Concierge-level package:
  • Space reservation to match your celebration: vibrant open-air patios, large banquet halls with floor-to-ceiling windows, ample natural light, contemporary furniture, high-end technology.
  • Vendor bidding for invitations, flowers, additional furniture and décor, entertainment, and more.
  • Planning and management of all details.
  • Coordination with your caterer and full-service bar staff.
  • Linens available at a minimal cost.
  • Limited inventory of complimentary centerpiece hardware (glassware, mirrors, etc.).
  • Complimentary event setup and take-down.
