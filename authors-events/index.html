Other Author Events

Spend an evening with these diverse authors and hear the stories behind their stories. We regularly host bestsellers, local authors, children's writers, and more.

Love in the Afternoon: Romance Author Panel

Parker, Saturday, June 16, 2PM

Celebrate romance and enjoy tea and author talks with a panel of romance authors, including Jennifer Turano, Carla Laureano, Sarah Hegger, Sara Richardson, Lisa Brown Roberts and Lisa Tawn Bergren. Books will also be sold. Adults.
Register Now

Sandra Dallas

Castle Rock, Saturday, June 16, 6:30PM

The award-winning and New York Times bestselling author will join us to talk about her new novel, The Patchwork Bride. Books will be sold at the event, and a book signing will follow the author's presentation. Adults.
Register Now

Craig Grossi & Fred

Parker, Wednesday, June 20, 6:30PM

The former U.S. Marine, along with his dog Fred, will join us to talk about their new book, Craig & Fred. It's an uplifting story of how they met on an Afghan battlefield and ended up rescuing each other. A book sale will follow the author's talk. Adults and kids ages 8+.
Register Now

Dr. Alan Stern

Highlands Ranch, Friday, June 22, 7PM

Explore Pluto with planetary scientist Dr. Alan Stern, who led NASA's New Horizons mission to Pluto. Stern will talk about the mission and his new book, Chasing New Horizons, followed by a book sale and signing. Teens/adults.
Register Now
