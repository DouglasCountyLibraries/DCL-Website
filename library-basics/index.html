Library Basics

Welcome! If you need a library card or are a new cardholder, explore some frequently asked account questions here. For quick access, add DCL.org to your mobile device's home screen.

Add DCL.org to Your Home Screen

iOS

  1. Open Safari and go to DCL.org.
  2. At the bottom of the screen, tap the share icon.
  3. Tap the "Add to Home Screen" icon.
  4. Enter a name for the homescreen icon and tap "Add."

Android

  1. Open Chrome and go to DCL.org.
  2. Tap the menu button, then "Add to Home Screen."
  3. Enter a name for the homescreen icon and tap "Add."

Fines, Fees & Borrowing

Print, Copy, Fax

Text Alerts

Text SIGNUP to dcl@shoutbomb.com to get account information via text. We’ll let you know when your holds are available for pickup, and when items are due or overdue. Standard texting rates apply.

Homebound Delivery

Qualified card holders can have library materials delivered right to their doors if they are unable to come to the library due to illness or disability.

Check Out Colorado

A limited number of backpacks containing a pass good for admission to any Colorado State Park are available for checkout from our welcome desks.

Adventure Pass

Our partnerships with area cultural institutions let you reserve free passes up to 30 days in advance.

DCL Hotspot

Borrow a DCL hotspot and connect up to 10 devices simultaneously to enjoy long-range Wi-Fi indoors or out.

Nontraditional Items

Check out a sewing machine, loom or spinning wheel starting in February. Items will be available on a first-come, first-served basis; ask a staff member for more information.

Fines, Fees & Borrowing

Most materials check out for three weeks. Magazines and DVDs under 200 minutes check out for one week, and each patron may have up to 99 physical items checked out at any one time. The late fee for most materials is 20 cents per day, with a three‑day grace period.

Wireless Printing

Print on the library's public printers during library hours regardless of device, network or location. Large documents and PDFs may take a few minutes to print; jobs not picked up within two hours of being sent will be deleted.

Printing & Copying

Letter and legal-size copies
  • Black and white: 10 cents per page; 20 cents per double-sided page
  • Color: 25 cents per page; 50 cents per double-sided page
11-inch by 17-inch copies
  • Black and white: 20 cents per page; 40 cents per double-sided page
  • Color: 50 cents per page; $1 per double-sided page
MasterCard and Visa are accepted, with a $1 minimum charge per transaction.

Faxing

The library sends faxes from the Highlands Ranch, Lone Tree, Parker, Castle Rock and Roxborough locations. The fee is $1.75 for the first page and $1 for each additional page. The library cannot accept incoming faxes and cannot send international faxes.
