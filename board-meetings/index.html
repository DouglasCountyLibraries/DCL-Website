Board Meetings

DCL regular library board meetings are held at least eight times a year at locations around the District. Board study sessions, committee meetings and such are held both on a recurring basis and/or as needed.

All meetings listed are open to the public. Public comment is heard at regular library board meetings and set public hearings.
