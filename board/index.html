Trustees are volunteers, appointed by the Douglas County Commissioners. The board adopts and oversees the budget, bylaws and policy. The board conducts long range planning and hires and evaluates the library director. Board meetings are held on the last Wednesday of every month at 7pm, at various library locations around the county.

2016 Board Meeting Schedule

DCL Board meetings are held the last Wednesday of each month at 7 pm, except November and December. Monthly Board study session meetings are held at 11:30 am on the Thursday before the Board meeting. These meetings are for discussion only and no formal action is taken. Foundation and committee meetings are typically scheduled -as needed- from 5:30-6:30 p.m. on the same dates as the Board meetings. Foundation Board updates are also provided at the regular DCL Board meetings.
