Colorado

Open Records Request

Douglas County Libraries considers all Colorado Open Records Act (CORA) requests to be a public record.

  • By submitting this request, you are invoking Colorado revised statute 24-72-201 et seq. and agree that:

    • Your request will be responded to in accordance with this statute.
    • There may be a charge for filling this request per statute, not to exceed $0.25/page and $33.58 per hour staff fee for time spent responding to and filling the request after the first hour.
    • You will be given an estimate of cost prior to the compiling of any records estimated to take over one hour of staff time.
    • If the request is estimated to cost staff time beyond one hour, a deposit is required to fill the request. Any deposit in excess of the actual staff time needed will be refunded.

  • FOR STAFF USE ONLY
