A Storybook Holiday

That Hungry

Little Bug

Join us at our Parker location for a celebration of spring and new beginnings inspired by Eric Carle’s beloved children’s book The Very Hungry Caterpillar.

Sunday, March 27, 8-11AM

Tickets: $20 Adults, $15 Ages 3-13
Ages 0-2 free with paying adult
Experience a delightful array of family-friendly entertainment and activities, including children’s Storytime, fascinating exhibits, bug-tastic crafts, fun photo ops, and more. “Shop” our garden-fresh farmers market for special recipe ingredients, and enjoy a catered brunch buffet, coffee and tea, sweet drinks, and mimosas. This event is ideal for families with kids ages 2-5, but all ages are welcome.
Buy Tickets
Adults ages 21+ may enjoy a selection of mimosas with the purchase of an alcohol wristband for an additional $12.
Customer Service: (303) 791-7323
