November 15, 2025-January 4, 2026
Enjoy a free, wonder-filled holiday experience for the whole family! Our Forest of Stories exhibit displays 175 decorated trees, all dressed up in stories—from favorite children's tales and classic literature, to crime thrillers, mysteries and more. Visit Booked for the Holidays for more holiday fun at DCL!
Forest of Stories Tree of the Week Contest
Featured Tree of the Week, December 8-14: Great Expectations at Highlands Ranch. Congratulations to our past winners, Nora, Kammy & Stephanie.
Enter for a chance to win tickets to an event at a local entertainment venue. One winner will be drawn and contacted each week, November 17, 2025, through January 4, 2026.
  • One decorated tree will be featured (Featured Tree) each Monday, starting November 17.
  • Find the Featured Tree at the specified library branch.
  • Look for the ornament on the Featured Tree with that week’s QR code.
  • Scan the QR code to enter into that week’s drawing.
  • Contest runs Monday-Sunday each week, with a new prize and drawing each week.
  • Contest Terms & Conditions.
