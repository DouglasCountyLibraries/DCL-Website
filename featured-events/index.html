Spend an evening with these diverse authors and hear the stories behind their stories. We regularly host bestsellers, local authors, children's writers, and more.
Laurie Marr Wasmund
Sunday, January 11, 2PM, Castle Rock
Meet the Douglas County author and hear about her family's deep ties to the Castle Rock area. She'll discuss her book, Catching It Lovely, based on her great-great grandmother whose family homesteaded in Castle Rock in the 1870s. Books will also be sold. ADULTS.
Register Now
Maria Gallucci
Sunday, February 8, 2PM, Highlands Ranch
Join us for an afternoon with Colorado author Maria Gallucci as she discusses her new memoir, Raised in Silence, a reflection on her upbringing as the child of Deaf parents. The author will share stories of navigating the unique worlds of a Deaf household and a hearing community, and how these experiences shaped her life. ADULTS.
