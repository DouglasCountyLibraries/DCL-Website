Author Events

Spend an evening with these diverse authors and hear the stories behind their stories. We regularly host bestsellers, local authors, children's writers, and more.

Sandra Dallas

Saturday, April 22, 6PM, Highlands Ranch

Don’t miss award-winning Colorado author Sandra Dallas when she joins us to talk about her new novel, "Where Coyotes Howl," a moving ode to rural life in early 20th-century Wyoming. Books will be sold at the event courtesy of Tattered Cover Book Store. ADULTS.
Join the Waitlist

Luis Alberto Urrea

Saturday, June 24, 6:30PM, Highlands Ranch

Enjoy a summer evening with bestselling author and Pulitzer Prize finalist Luis Alberto Urrea. The author will join us to talk about his new novel, "Good Night, Irene," a story of women’s heroism during World War II, inspired by his mother’s Red Cross service. Books will be sold at the event courtesy of Tattered Cover Book Store. ADULTS.
Register Now

Lisa See

Wednesday, June 28, 6:30PM, Highlands Ranch

Join New York Times bestselling author Lisa See and hear about her new historical novel, "Lady Tan’s Circle of Women," inspired by the true story of a woman physician from 15th-century China. Perfect for fans of "The Island of Sea Women" and "Snow Flower and the Secret Fan." Books will also be sold at the event. ADULTS.
