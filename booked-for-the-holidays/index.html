HOLIDAYS

Celebrate a Season of Stories at DCL

Jingle all the way to DCL for our annual winter holiday events each November and December. Our popular holiday series includes Forest of Stories, our classic Storybook Holiday celebration, festive singalongs, visits with Santa, and more. At DCL, the holidays are booked for you!
FOrEST of STORIeS
November 16, 2024-January 4, 2025 at all DCL locations!
Enjoy a wonder-filled experience for the whole family! Our expanded Forest of Stories exhibit displays 175 decorated trees, all dressed up in stories—from favorite children's tales and classic literature, to crime thrillers, mysteries and more. There is something for everyone!
Presented by Douglas County Libraries Foundation

A StoRybOOk HOLiday
Each year, during the first weekend in December, we celebrate a classic children's story at our dinner and brunch events. Guests enjoy a delicious menu, fun photo ops, festive crafts, make-and-take-gifts, and delightful holiday entertainment.
Sign up for our newsletter to get the inside scoop on next year's event!

SING-ALoNG
Our interactive, Storytime-like sing-alongs in December feature live music and are ideal for families with children ages 2-5.
COcoa & CaRoling
Our evening events in November and December are perfect for the whole family! Sip on hot cocoa, sing along to favorite holiday tunes, and enjoy the magic of the season among our Forest of Stories and on Castle Rock's Aloha Plaza West, sponsored by Aloha Fund.
A Visit with Santa
Always popular, these special early-December events include classic holiday stories, activities, snacks, and a visit with Santa.
