Celebrate a Season of Stories at DCL

Jingle all the way to DCL for magical holiday experiences this winter. From the most wonderful Forest of Stories, to a Storybook Holiday celebration, to singalongs and Santa, the halls are decked and the holidays are booked for you! Join us for unforgettable moments and memories this season.
FOrEST of STORIeS
Nov. 19-Jan. 6 | All DCL Locations
Enjoy a wonder-filled experience for the whole family! Our expanded Forest of Stories exhibit displays 150 decorated trees, all dressed up in stories—from favorite children's tales and classic literature, to crime thrillers, mysteries and more. There is something for everyone!
