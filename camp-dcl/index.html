Camp DCL

HoliDays Camps

December 20-22, 9AM-4PM

Kids will have the most wonderful time at our winter break camps in Lone Tree and Parker!

If your child enjoys cooking, building with LEGOs, creating works of art, holiday crafting, or science and bots, you’ll find a fun camp here.
Please bring your own nut-free lunch and water bottle. Snacks and all needed materials and supplies will be provided.

Lone Tree

Cooking & LEGO Camp

December 20, Ages 5-10, $75
Kids will love this festive day of fun! We’ll start the day exploring exciting seasonal tastes (all as local as possible) and mastering culinary skills that can be used at home. In the afternoon, we’ll re-create memorable moments from Dr. Seuss’s classic Grinch story with LEGOs. Campers will work together to build Whoville using LEGO bricks and mini-figures, as well as a sled or catapult for the Grinch to use. We’ll also play games, sing holiday songs, make LEGO ornaments to bring home, and craft a gift or two to place under the tree for loved ones. Facilitated by professionals from Sticky Fingers Cooking and Snapology. Bring your own nut-free lunch and water bottle.

FAQs

Please note: Your camper's spot is not fully secured until your payment has been processed by Douglas County Libraries and you have received a payment confirmation email.
Q: Are snacks provided, and what are they?
A: Yes, we provide a drink (usually juice and/or water), fresh fruit (an apple, small orange, etc.), and usually a nut-free prepackaged snack (such as graham crackers, animal crackers, etc.). We will let you know which specific snacks will be offered for your camp in the pre-camp email.
Q: Will you provide more information on the camp as the date gets closer?
A: Yes, a pre-camp email will be sent the week prior to your scheduled camp and it will list the dates, times, location, a brief daily description, tips or notes for the week, a contact phone number, and any additional information you'll need to review prior to the start of camp.
Q: What is your cancellation policy?
A: Cancellations & Refunds: Cancellations made up to three business days prior to Camp start will be eligible for a full refund, less a $50 cancellation fee. No refund is given for a cancellation with fewer than three business days' notice. A full refund will be given if DCL cancels a Camp for any reason. In the event of a DCL-initiated cancellation, parents or guardians will be notified by email or phone. Parents wishing to withdraw a child from Camp should provide written notification via email.
