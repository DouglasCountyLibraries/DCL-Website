Kid-friendly Fun:

Little Letters Post Office

The library’s interactive play space features fun aspects of a real-life post office, including a standalone mail delivery truck. Kids can process and deliver mail using their imaginations and provided play materials, equipment, and devices. They can also dress up in various postal employee uniforms to accept packages and mail, weigh the items, ring them up on the cash register, place them on the conveyor belt, and sort them into mail slots.
