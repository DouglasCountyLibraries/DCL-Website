12 Days of Making Wishes Come True

These are some of the ways Douglas County Libraries Foundation makes wishes come true in our community.

Day 4: Kids can be anything they want to be.

Celia dreams of being a chef so she can cook for her dad. Griffin wants to build skyscrapers high into the sky. And Mia hopes to solve the case of her disappearing socks. If only there were a way to feed these youthful ambitions.
There is, and it's called Camp DCL. Our playful, educational day camps make learning fun for kids ages 6-12, allowing them to experience what it would be like to fulfill their ambitions. And thanks to generous donors, we offer camp scholarships for those who meet the requirements.
Wish come true:
At Camp DCL, kids can be anything they want to be.
