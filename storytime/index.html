Storytime

Join us Monday-Saturday for Storytime fun and activities!

Library staff will lead songs, rhymes, reading and fingerplays to keep nurturing those all-important emerging reading skills.
View the Storytime Calendar to find a Storytime at your library. Thank you for keeping Storytime allergen-free.

Virtual Storytime
Early literacy is what children know about reading and writing before they can actually read or write. Storytime nurtures six important pre-reading skills:
  • Narrative skills
  • Phonological awareness
  • Letter knowledge
  • Print motivation
  • Vocabulary
  • Print awareness
Storytime at Douglas County Libraries emphasizes five simple activities — reading, talking, writing, singing and playing—that help children learn the skills to be ready to read. You can build on your child's learning by incorporating these activities into your day as well. A great way to do this is to sign up for Dolly Parton's Imagination Library. Exposure to lots of books is a great predictor of reading readiness and school success.
