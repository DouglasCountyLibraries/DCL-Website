The Jungle Book

Friday, September 27, 2019, 6-11PM

CU South Denver

Purchase Tickets Now
"A brave heart and a courteous tongue, said he. They shall carry thee far through the Jungle." -Rudyard Kipling, The Jungle Book
You’re invited to join the Douglas County Libraries Foundation as we forge through the jungle of the late, great Rudyard Kipling’s imagination! We’ll provide much more than the bare necessities for an unforgettable evening, and you’ll enjoy delightful adventures amid the chatter of jungle creatures and the lush beauty of the forested heart of sun-warmed India.
Your generous sponsorship ensures that your guests have a fun and exciting evening, but it also does so much more! Your dollars help fund important library programs that reach youth, adults and families in Douglas County and across Colorado, and help ensure the long-term success of our outstanding libraries, which are critical to our community’s education, workforce development and economic development.
Come to the gala in your favorite theme-inspired outfit—or not—more casual attire is perfectly fine! Enjoy a signature cocktail, a delicious catered dinner and a special dessert. Talk with our special guests, revel in remarkable entertainment, and experience the fun of a silent auction, where you just might find the treasure of your dreams. Leave with the wonderful memory of a favorite childhood tale, knowing that because of your support, your libraries will continue to be great places for young and old—where all can enjoy epic journeys of self-discovery.

See below for a list of the amazing benefits of adding your name to our list of prestigious sponsors!
