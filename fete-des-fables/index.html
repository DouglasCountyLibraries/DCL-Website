Fête des Fables Gala

Beauty and the Beast

"To see you was instantly to love you. Entering your apartment, tremblingly, my joy was excessive to find that you could behold me with greater intrepidity than I could behold myself."
—Gabrielle-Suzanne Barbot de Villeneuve
Join the Douglas County Libraries Foundation to celebrate the classic story of true love and its timeless messages of inner beauty and the transformative nature of kindness.
You’ll enjoy an unforgettable evening in the style of provincial France, including beautiful scenery, a signature cocktail, catered dinner and dessert, remarkable entertainment, and a magnificent silent auction, where you just might find the treasure of your dreams.
Friday, September 19, Highlands Ranch Mansion
Tickets: $130
Event proceeds benefit the Douglas County Libraries Foundation.
