11th Annual
Fête des Fables Gala

Snow
White

"Over the seven jeweled hills, beyond the seventh wall, in the cottage of the seven dwarfs, dwells Snow White, fairest one of all."
—Snow White
Step beyond the familiar fairy tale and into the shadowed forest of our most dramatic gala yet.
Join the Douglas County Libraries Foundation for an evening of exquisite dining, immersive entertainment, and moments of enchantment inspired by the timeless tale of beauty, envy and power. Your evening in the velvet-dark wood features a custom-crafted cocktail, sumptuous meal, striking entertainment, and a tempting silent auction, where hidden treasures await those bold enough to claim them.
Friday, September 25, 6PM, Highlands Ranch Mansion
Tickets: $175
Event proceeds benefit the Douglas County Libraries Foundation.
