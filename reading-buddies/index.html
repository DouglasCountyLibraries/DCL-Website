Reading Buddies

Reading Buddies is a reading enrichment program for children in grades 1-3, pairing a trained teen Big Buddy with a struggling young reader.

Little Buddies

Applications for Little Buddies are accepted through February 2 or until all positions are filled. Sessions will run from March 4-May 2.
  • Reading Buddies is not a tutoring service. Please attend all scheduled meetings.
  • You will need a Douglas County Libraries library card. Get a card if you don’t already have one.
  • Upon acceptance to the Reading Buddies program, your Little Buddy will be paired with a Big Buddy, a teen library volunteer who is trained to read with kids in a way that helps encourage a love of reading.
  • Space is limited and your participation in the program is not guaranteed until all applications have been reviewed and we have been able to match your child with a Teen Big Buddy volunteer.
Apply Now

Big Buddies

Applications for the Spring session open soon.
  • Buddies meet weekly for two months.
  • For each session, spend 30 minutes preparing, followed by one hour of reading and literacy games with your Little Buddy.
  • Volunteer spots are limited and your participation in the program is not guaranteed until your application has been received and approved.
  • Volunteer orientation and training are required.
  • When applications become available, you may apply by selecting a library location.
