Camp DCL

Summer Camps
June 8-August 7, 2026 | Ages 5-12

Explore, create, and experiment this summer at Camp DCL! Choose from our weeklong half-day camps at six DCL locations or full-day camps at select locations.

Materials and snacks are provided for all camps; full-day campers must bring a nut-free lunch daily. Please also bring your own water bottle.
Frequently Asked Questions
Castle Pines
STEAM Challenge LEGO Camp
June 15-19*, 9AM-12PM
Ages 5-10, $200
Ready for a Survivor-style challenge? Campers will put their design, building, and critical-thinking skills to the test through daily design challenges with a teammate. Earn Survivor Bucks for performing well and then use those bucks to purchase supplies or prizes throughout camp week. With encouragement, young builders will carefully work through the engineering design process to see how often they can design with a purpose. Facilitated by professionals from Snapology. Bring your own water bottle.
*Camp will operate on schedule on June 19, despite DCL's Juneteenth closure.
Fizz-Bang-Boom Science Camp
July 20-24, 9AM-12PM
Ages 5-10, $250
Take the physics of motion, add electrifying experiments, and mix it up with some chemistry for a weeklong blast! Budding scientists will create their own battery-powered invention, experiment with light and flight, make their own kaleidoscope, investigate giant floating bubbles, and more! Facilitated by professionals from Science Matters. Bring your own water bottle.
On sale for staff only, Sunday, February 1!
Castle Rock
Saddle Up & Create Art Camp
June 8-12, 9AM-12PM
Ages 5-10, $225
Giddy up—the spirit of the frontier meets the golden glow of autumn, farm animals, and colorful harvest! Young artists will paint rolling fields, craft cowboy hats and horses, and sculpt pumpkins, corn and playful scarecrows using a vibrant range of materials, including clay, acrylics, pastels, and natural objects. Each project celebrates the colors and traditions of the West while fostering imagination, artistic skills, and a sense of adventure. Facilitated by professionals from KidzArt. Bring your own water bottle.
Claws, Codes & Constellations Science Camp
July 6-10, 9AM-12PM
Ages 5-10, $250
Blast off into a week of discovery, creativity, and hands-on fun! Campers will crack secret codes, become planet protectors, test Newton’s laws through games and sports, explore the wild wonders of nature, and train like astronauts on a space adventure. Every day brings exciting new activities, from making recycled paper to creating animal tracks to experimenting with gravity, all while learning by doing. Facilitated by professionals from Mad Science. Bring your own water bottle.
Castle Rock Full Day Camp
LEGO & Cooking
July 27-31, 9AM-4PM
Ages 5-10, $425
Pack your global curiosity! Campers will start each day discovering unique aspects of different cultures, like national flags, traditional cuisines, influential artists, and historical landmarks, and work with a partner to solve challenges in various "countries." Kids will learn about world cultures and compete in fun challenges to test their building and problem-solving skills. After lunch, we’ll take a culinary journey around the world, traveling from continent to continent through the kitchen, cooking up dishes inspired by countries near and far. Facilitated by professionals from Snapology and Sticky Fingers. Bring your own nut-free lunch and water bottle each day.
On sale for staff only, Sunday, February 1!

June 8-12, 1-4PM
Ages 5-10, $250
Join us in the kitchen, where science sizzles! Young chefs will transform the kitchen into a STEAM playground, exploring science, technology, engineering, art and math through food. Every recipe is a tasty experiment waiting to be discovered. Facilitated by professionals from Sticky Fingers. Bring your own water bottle.
Colorful Colorado Art Camp
June 22-26, 9AM-12PM
Ages 5-10, $225

Guided by award-winning artists who love teaching kids, campers will have fun drawing, painting and sculpting based on our state, while also problem-solving and developing critical-thinking skills. Highlands Ranch Full Day Camp
LEGO & Art
July 13-17, 9AM-4PM
Ages 5-10, $425
Have a blast, from the design lab to the art studio! Campers will start each day sharpening their design and building skills by learning how to take their ideas and turn them into real-world buildings. Instructors will guide campers through the building process, from creating a floor plan to disaster-proofing, to create super LEGO architecture. July 27-31, 1-4PM
Ages 8-12, $225

But Merida chooses to defy her mother and tradition, and follows the ancient forest wisps to seek help from an eccentric witch who grants her an ill-fated wish. LEGO & Art

FAQs
Please note: Your camper's spot is not fully secured until your payment has been processed by Douglas County Libraries and you have received a payment confirmation email.
Q: Are snacks provided, and what are they?
A: Yes, we provide a drink (usually juice and/or water), fresh fruit (an apple, small orange, etc.), and usually a nut-free prepackaged snack (such as graham crackers, animal crackers, etc.). We will let you know which specific snacks will be offered for your camp in the pre-camp email.
Q: Will you provide more information on the camp as the date gets closer?
A: Yes, a pre-camp email will be sent the week prior to your scheduled camp and it will list the dates, times, location, a brief daily description, tips or notes for the week, a contact phone number, and any additional information you'll need to review prior to the start of camp.
Q: What is your cancellation policy?
A: Cancellations & Refunds: Cancellations made up to three business days prior to Camp start will be eligible for a full refund, less a $25 cancellation fee. No refund is given for a cancellation with fewer than three business days' notice. A full refund will be given if DCL cancels a Camp for any reason. In the event of a DCL-initiated cancellation, parents or guardians will be notified by email or phone. Parents wishing to withdraw a child from Camp should provide written notification via email.
Please read our Camp DCL Policies & Procedures for more information.
