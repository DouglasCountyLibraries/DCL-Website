Castle Pines Event Hall

Your event is reserved under our Self-Service package. You are responsible for setup, resetting the space per the floor plan, and connecting any A/V equipment following the provided instructions. Visit the nearest service desk for more assistance. Review terms and conditions at dcl.org/meeting-room-guidelines. To inquire about additional services, contact the Event + Hospitality department at (303) 688-7605.

Reseting the Floor Plan:

Using the Presentation System:

  1. Locate the wall control panel.
  2. Touch the screen to begin.
  3. Enter the password "1234".
  4. Select your presentation source:
    • Floor Box
      • Connect your device with a cable to the port in the floor box closest to the podium. If no cables are available, ask a staff member for assistance.
    • Wireless (AirMedia)
      1. Connect your device to "wireless.dclibraries".
      2. Follow the on-screen instructions to cast using the Creston AirMedia app:
      • Personal Computer: Open a browser, enter the IP address shown, download and run the AirMedia application and enter the 4-digit code.
      • Mobile Device: Download the Crestron AirMedia app for iOS or Android, open it and enter the 4-digit code.
    • Blu-ray Player
      • Locate the Blu-ray player, power it on and insert your disc. Ask a staff member for assistance if needed.
  5. Use the up and down arrows on the wall control panel to adjust volume.
  6. End your session
    1. Press "Clear Source" on the wall control panel.
    2. Press the power button to turn off the system.
    3. Return any borrowed cables or adapters to a staff member.
