Lone Tree Event Hall

Your event is reserved under our Self-Service package. You are responsible for setup, resetting the space per the floor plan, and connecting any A/V equipment following the provided instructions. Visit the nearest service desk for more assistance. Review terms and conditions at dcl.org/meeting-room-guidelines.

Resetting the Floor Plan:

Using the Presentation System:

  1. Locate the wall control panel
  2. Touch the screen to begin
  3. Enter the password "1234". If you are using both Event Halls, ask a staff member for assistance in combining the screens.
  4. Select your presentation source
    • Wall Plate:
      • Connect your Apple or other device with a cable to the port in the wall plate closest to the podium. If no cables are available, ask a staff member for assistance. Note: Customers are encouraged to bring their own adapters specific to their devices.
    • Blu-ray Player:
      • Locate the Blu-ray player, power it on and insert your disc. Ask a staff member for assistance if needed.
    • Wireless Casting from a PC or Compatible Mobile Devices*:
      • Connect your device to DCL's public wireless network, "wireless.dclibraries". Follow the on-screen instructions to cast using the Crestron AirMedia app:
      • PC: Open a browser, enter the IP address shown on the presentation screen, download and run the Crestron AirMedia app and enter the 4-digit code
      • Mobile Devices: Download the Crestron AirMedia app from your device's app store, open the app and enter the 4-digit code shown on the presentation screen
      *Apple iOS devices are not supported. Compatibility with other mobile devices depends on the operating system, device model, and OS version.
  5. Using a microphone:
    • Locate a handheld or lavalier microphone
    • Power it on by holding the power button or sliding the power switch
    • Warning: Do not change the channel on the microphone, as it may prevent it from working with the sound system
  6. Adjust the volume from the control panel using the up and down arrows
  7. Ending your session:
    • Press "Clear Source" on the wall control panel
    • Press the power button to turn off the system
    • Return any borrowed cables or adapters to a staff member
Thank you for trusting Douglas County Libraries for your event needs. To inquire about additional services, contact the Event + Hospitality department at (303) 688-7605.

Resetting the Floor Plan:

Using the A/V Cart:

  1. Ensure the cart is plugged in and power on the screen

  3. Select the corresponding button for HDMI or Blu-ray
  4. Press the power button to turn off the screen
  5. Return any borrowed cables or adapters to a staff member
