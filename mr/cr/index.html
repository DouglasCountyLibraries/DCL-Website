Castle Rock Event Hall A

Your event is reserved under our Self-Service package. You are responsible for setup, resetting the space per the floor plan of Event Hall A or Event Hall A & B, and connecting any A/V equipment following the provided instructions. Visit the nearest service desk for more assistance. Review terms and conditions at dcl.org/meeting-room-guidelines.

Floor Plan for Resetting Event Hall A:

Floor Plan for Resetting Event Hall A & B:

Using the Presentation System:

  1. Locate the wall control panel next to the door
  2. Touch the screen to begin
  3. Enter the password "1234". If you are using both Event Halls, ask a staff member for assistance in combining the screens.
  4. Select your presentation source
    • Wall Plate:
      • Connect your device with a cable to the port in the wall plate near the screen. If no cables are available, ask a staff member for assistance. Note: Customers are encouraged to bring their own adapters specific to their devices.
    • Wireless Casting from a Laptop or Compatible Mobile Devices*:
      • Connect your device to DCL's public wireless network, "wireless.dclibraries". Download the Crestron AirMedia app from your device's app store. Follow the step by step instructions to cast or mirror your device to the presentation screen.
      *Compatibility with mobile devices depends on the operating system, device model, and OS version.
  5. Using a microphone:
    • Locate a handheld or lavalier microphone
    • Power it on by holding the power button or sliding the power switch
    • Warning: Do not change the channel on the microphone, as it may prevent it from working with the sound system
  6. Adjust the volume from the control panel using the up and down arrows
  7. Ending your session:
    • Press "Clear Source" on the wall control panel
    • Press the power button to turn off the system
    • Return any borrowed cables or adapters to a staff member
Thank you for trusting Douglas County Libraries for your event needs. To inquire about additional services, contact the Event + Hospitality department at (303) 688-7605.
