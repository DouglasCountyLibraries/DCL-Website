Highlands Ranch Event Hall A

Your event is reserved under our Self-Service package. You are responsible for setup, resetting the space per the floor plan of Event Hall A or Event Hall A & B, and connecting any A/V equipment following the provided instructions. Visit the nearest service desk for more assistance. Review terms and conditions at dcl.org/meeting-room-guidelines. To inquire about additional services, contact the Event + Hospitality department at (303) 688-7605.

Resetting the Floor Plan of Event Hall A:

Resetting the Floor Plan of Event Hall A & B:

Using the Presentation System:

  1. Locate the wall control panel.
  2. Touch the screen to begin.
  3. Enter the password "1234". If you are using both Event Halls, ask a staff member for assistance in combining the screens.
  4. Select your presentation source:
    • Wall Plate
      • Connect your Apple or other device with a cable to the port in the wall plate closest to the podium. If no cables are available, ask a staff member for assistance.
    • Wireless (AirMedia for PC and Android)
      1. Connect your device to DCL's public wireless network, "wireless.dclibraries".
      2. Follow the on-screen instructions to cast using the Crestron AirMedia app:
      • PC: Open a browser, enter the IP address shown on the presentation screen, download and run the Crestron AirMedia app and enter the 4-digit code.
      • Android: Download the Crestron AirMedia app for Android, open it and enter the 4-digit code.
    • Blu-ray Player
      • Locate the Blu-ray player, power it on and insert your disc. Ask a staff member for assistance if needed.
  5. Use a microphone:
    1. Locate a handheld or lavalier microphone.
    2. Power it on by holding the power button or sliding the power switch.
    3. Warning: Do not change the channel on the microphone, as it may prevent it from working with the sound system.
  6. Use the up and down arrows on the wall control panel to adjust volume.
  7. End your session:
    1. Press "Clear Source" on the wall control panel.
    2. Press the power button to turn off the system.
    3. Return any borrowed cables or adapters to a staff member.
