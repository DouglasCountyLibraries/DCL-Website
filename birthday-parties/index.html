Birthday Parties at DCL

Celebrate your child's birthday at Douglas County Libraries!

Available Saturdays and Sundays, 2:30-4:30PM; $195 for up to 12 children; add $15 for each additional child. Some restrictions apply.

Birthday Parties at DCL

Restrictions:

  • One adult chaperone (18 or older) must be present for every four children present.
  • Reservations must be made at least 14 days before the requested event date.
  • For additional d√©cor, food and beverage, standard room terms and conditions apply.

We offer three fun themes!

Our customized parties are for kids in age groups 0-5 and 6-12. Each party package includes:

  • Setup, cleanup and a dedicated party coordinator.
  • A custom invitation that you may email to your guests.
  • Age-appropriate activities (20-30 minutes).
  • A free book for the birthday boy/girl.
  • Your choice of 12 individual cookies or one giant cookie from Eileen's Colossal Cookies. Plates and napkins provided.
  • Twelve (12) age-appropriate goody bags.
  • Twelve (12) thank-you notes for the birthday boy/girl's use after the party.
