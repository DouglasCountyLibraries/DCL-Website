Birthday Parties at DCL

Celebrate your child's birthday at Douglas County Libraries!

$225 for up to 12 children (the birthday girl/boy + 11 friends); add $15 for each additional child. Some restrictions apply.

Our customized parties are for kids ages 2-7. Each party package includes:

  • Your choice of three fun themes: Once Upon a Time, Things that Roar, Folklore and Fantasy
  • Setup, cleanup and a dedicated party coordinator
  • A fun themed invitation that you may email to your guests
  • Age-appropriate activities (1.5 hours)
  • A free book for the birthday boy/girl
  • A lollipop bouquet
  • Twelve (12) age-appropriate goody bags
  • Twelve (12) thank-you notes for the birthday boy/girl's use after the party
Get Started
Have questions? Call Talayna Akrie, Events & Hospitality Services Manager, at (303) 688-7605.

View Event Spaces

Restrictions:

  • One adult chaperone (18 or older) must be present for every four children present.
  • Reservations must be made at least 14 business days before the requested event date.
  • Birthday party packages are structured for one birthday girl/boy. To celebrate more than one child at a single party, add $50.
  • For additional décor, food and beverage, standard room terms and conditions apply.
Customer Service: (303) 791-7323
