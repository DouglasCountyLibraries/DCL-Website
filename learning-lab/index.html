DCL Learning Lab

Welcome to the DCL Learning Lab! These professional, supported services are designed for all levels of students, from elementary to high school, and offer opportunities to study, experiment and learn in a non-school environment.

Camp DCL: Enrichment Series

This series of traveling mini-camps is designed for elementary students ages 5-10 and takes place at various DCL locations. Each session focuses on enrichment beyond the basic school subjects, giving kids a chance to get hands-on with learning and rounding out their educational experience.

Science of Superpowers with LEGOs

Ages 5-10, $30
Explore the worlds of your favorite superheroes! Students will learn about forces, energy, and problem-solving as they build models inspired by Superman, Batgirl, and all their favorite heroes. Experimentation and fun are crucial components of this “super” educational program. Facilitated by professionals from Snapology. Please bring a nut-free snack and water bottle.
  • Castle Rock, TUE, JAN 5, 2:30-5:30PM
  • Highlands Ranch, WED, JAN 13, 2:30-5:30PM
  • Lone Tree, THU, JAN 21, 2:30-5:30PM
  • Parker, MON, JAN 25, 2:30-5:30PM

Study Hall + Student Support

These supervised, DCL-supported work sessions are designed with the classic study hall in mind and are geared to kids in middle and high school. Students may work on their school projects and studies in a dedicated library setting, and may also receive professional support when needed from staff and partners with subject expertise as follows. Please schedule only one session per student per day.
  • Castle Rock: STEM subjects, WED & THU, 1-3PM
  • Highlands Ranch: Language Arts, WED & THU, 1-3PM
  • Parker: STEM subjects, MON & TUE, 1-3PM
