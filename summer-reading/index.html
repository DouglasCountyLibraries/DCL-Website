Giddyup Giveaways

Sign up for Summer Reading* and you could ride off with more than great stories!
All Howdy, Partner – Let's Read participants are automatically entered into our random prize drawing, featuring two exciting prize packages: the Grand Prize Lucky Lasso package and the Second in the Saddle Runner-up package. Register now for your chance to win!
Grand Prize Lucky Lasso Prize Package
  • Four tickets to the National Western Stock Show in January 2027. Winner can choose from four different rodeos or an evening of dancing horses. Tickets include grounds admission for the day of the chosen event.
  • Four General Admission tickets good for one day during the Douglas County Fair & Rodeo.
  • Four Reserved Seats for the July 31 PRCA Rodeo at the Douglas County Fair & Rodeo, including grounds admission.
  • Eight-person game show experience at Game Show Battle Rooms plus prize pack.
  • $100 Hickory House restaurant gift card.
  • One autographed C.J. Box book.
  • Kids card games set: Old Maid, Crazy Eights, Memory Match, Slap Jack, War; four-pack Little Hands card holders; two standard decks of playing cards; dominoes; and two books on card games: "Super Fun Family Card Games" and "Best of Classic Card Games."
Second in the Saddle Runner-up Prize Package
  • Four tickets to the National Western Stock Show in January 2027. Winner can choose from four different rodeos or an evening of dancing horses. Tickets include grounds admission for the day of the chosen event.

Summer Reading Prize Pickup Kickoff
Visit the Summer Reading table at any DCL location on June 6 from 10 a.m.-2 p.m. to receive a special, limited-edition prize, while supplies last; available June 6 only when you show your Summer Reading email confirmation or Beanstack registration badge.
You may also pick up sign-up prizes and reading logs, while supplies last. Registration for prize pickup is not required.
Contest Rules and Restrictions
Contest applies only to K-12 students in schools located in Douglas County and accredited by the Colorado Department of Education. Awards will be given to one school at each level: elementary, middle, high, and other (e.g., charter, K-12).
Sign-ups are tallied through Beanstack online registration. The school in each category with the most sign-ups as a percentage of 2025-2026 enrollment earns the award.
Contest runs May 1-July 31. Prizes courtesy of Douglas County Libraries Foundation. Award recipients will be notified in August 2026.
