2025 Summer Fun for All Ages

Saturday, May 31-Thursday, July 31

Summer reading is abuzz with exciting activities, prizes, and giveaways.
We’ve suggested 1,000 minutes of reading as a worthy goal, but if this challenge isn’t right for you or your family, feel free to create your own challenge. You can increase or decrease the number of minutes or set a goal for the number of books or pages you want to read.

Bug Out of Town Giveaways

We’re giving away three $500 Southwest Airlines gift cards to participants who sign up to read. Register now for Don’t Bug Me, I’m Reading for your chance to win!
Everyone registered May 1-July 31 will be entered into the random prize drawings. Drawings will take place in early August. Winners will be notified individually and announced via DCL’s digital channels (first name, last initial). No purchase necessary and registration is free.

Prizes

Prizes available May 31-August 7, or while supplies last.

Sign-Up Prizes

Visit any DCL location to pick up your Summer Reading sign-up prizes, while supplies last.
  • Babies 0-2: Bubbles
  • Kids 3-12: Bug Viewer and Raising Cane's Free Kids Meal
  • Teens & Adults: One Free Item from Second Chapter Bookstore (up to $3 value)
  • Registered participants are also eligible for discounted tickets to select Colorado Rapids and Colorado Rockies games.

Finishing Prizes

Available starting June 2, while supplies last. Collect a prize when you complete your challenge, or just for having a fun summer!
  • Babies & Kids (Ages 0-12): One Free New Book
  • Teens & Adults: Reusable Canvas Tote

Goals

Help us meet our community goal of 10 million minutes read!

Add your minutes to help us meet our goal.

If we meet the community goal, the Douglas County Libraries Foundation will donate $1,000 to the Colorado Agricultural Leadership Foundation (CALF), whose mission is to connect people of all ages and abilities to agriculture through educational programs, community projects, and leadership opportunities.

Help your school earn $500!*

All you have to do is sign up (and remind your friends and classmates to sign up, too!), making sure you include your school's name and your grade when you register. The school with the most sign-ups by July 31 will be awarded $500. Award recipients announced in August. See contest rules and restrictions.

Summer Reading Kickoff Prize Pickup
Visit the Summer Reading table at any DCL location on May 31 from 10 a.m.-2 p.m. to receive a special, limited-edition prize, while supplies last; available May 31 only when you show your Summer Reading email confirmation or Beanstack registration badge. You may also pick up sign-up prizes and reading logs, while supplies last. Registration for prize pickup is not required.
Contest Rules and Restrictions
Contest applies only to K-12 students in schools located in Douglas County and accredited by the Colorado Department of Education. Awards will be given to one school at each level: elementary, middle, high, and other (e.g., charter, K-12).
Sign-ups are tallied through Beanstack online registration. The school in each category with the most sign-ups as a percentage of 2024-2025 enrollment earns the award.
Contest runs May 1-July 31. Prizes courtesy of Douglas County Libraries Foundation. Award recipients will be notified in August 2025.
