10 Days of Giveaways

June 4-13

Sign up to win!
Look what we have up our sleeve! We’re giving away 105 prizes to Summer Reading participants who sign up to read. What could you win? Unlock the Magic of Summer Reading and find out!
Each giveaway day features a random drawing for that day’s prize. Everyone registered May 1-July 15 will be entered into the prize drawings. Most prizes mailed directly to winners or delivery will be otherwise arranged. Winners will be announced via DCL’s digital channels (first name, last initial). No purchase necessary and registration is free.

Contest Rules and Restrictions

Contest applies only to K-12 students in schools located in Douglas County and accredited by the Colorado Department of Education. Awards will be given to one school at each level: elementary, middle, high, and other (e.g., charter, K-12).
Sign-ups are tallied through Beanstack online registration. The school in each category with the most sign-ups as a percentage of 2021-2022 enrollment earns the award.
Contest runs May 1-July 31. Prizes courtesy of Douglas County Libraries Foundation. Award recipients will be notified in August 2022.
