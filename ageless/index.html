Ageless Adults

DCL offers an array of services, events, and opportunities to connect for adults of all ages, because curiosity never retires.

Materials Delivery
Residents in participating communities may request book delivery. DCL also provides homebound delivery for qualified cardholders who are unable to visit the library due to illness or disability.
Connect!
Meet and connect with your community at the library. Pop in for Coffee Chat each Tuesday morning, attend a Book Lovers event, join a book club, request a Tech Tutoring session, and more.
Ageless Classes & Programs
Attend noncredit courses and special programs for the joy of learning! You’ll find classes and events to suit a wide array of interests, presented by expert partners and hosted at DCL locations.
Volunteer
Use your skills, give back to your community, and meet new people of all ages when you volunteer at DCL. We would love to work with you!
Memory Care Kits
Our specialized, self-contained kits for caregivers contain materials to help those struggling with memory issues. Kits check out for three weeks.
Book Clubs & Book Club Express
Join other book lovers to read and talk about books at a DCL-hosted book club, or reserve a handy to-go kit with curated resources for your own book club.
Nontraditional Items
Did you know you can check out a variety of gadgets, electronics, and other useful items that aren’t just books or DVDs? Take a look!
Online Learning
Keep growing! It’s simple and convenient to learn a new language, take DIY crafting classes, and hone your software and technology skills using your library card.

Connect!

DCL offers much more than books! Our events, programs, and services provide a deeper library experience to help you connect with your community.
Enjoy coffee, conversation, and games weekly at Coffee Chat. Join a DCL book club to read and discuss books in a group setting. Enjoy lively book talks with other avid readers at Book Lovers. Feed your curiosity in noncredit classes and courses. And keep up with the technology that keeps you connected to family and friends through individualized tech tutoring. Welcome!
