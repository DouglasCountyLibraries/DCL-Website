Discover Exceptional Event Spaces

Our three brand-new, vibrant buildings offer beautiful and ample event spaces, and maintain the high standards of service you may have encountered in our legacy buildings. From modern furniture to high-end technology, discover the ideal event space for your next corporate function or social event. Study Rooms

Study rooms may be reserved for up to two hours per week with capacity for eight people or less, depending on the room size. Study room sizes and accommodations vary from library to library. For help requesting a study room, please call (303) 791-7323 or visit your local library.

Parker Recording Studio

The Recording Studio can be reserved for up to three hours at a time. Room use can be extended if no one is waiting. Three single-user computer stations, located just outside the Recording Studio, can also be reserved for digital projects. Software on these computers and the Recording Studio computer may include:
  • Tinkercad
  • Sketch-up Pro
  • Blender
  • Unity
  • Adobe Creative Cloud
  • Final Cut Pro
  • Compressor 4
  • Motion 5
  • iMovie
  • Garageband
  • iWorks
  • Manga Studio 5
  • Camtasia Studio (Mac)
  • Logic Pro X
  • MainStage 3
Due to large demand, the Recording Studio and additional computer stations may be given to other patrons if you are more than 15 minutes late for your reserved time. The Recording Studio and additional computers can be booked online or via library staff on a walk-in basis. Need help? Please schedule your session at least five business days in advance for one-on-one assistance. We are not able to accommodate last-minute assistance requests at this time.

Customer Service: (303) 791-7323
