Academic Search Premier

Full-text article database of scholarly and peer-reviewed journals covering a variety of subjects, including biology, chemistry, psychology and physics.

American History Online

More than 500 years of American political, military, social and cultural history.

Ancient and Medieval History Online

The full scope of ancient world history, from prehistory through the mid-15th century, including biographies, events and topics, primary sources, time-lines, images, videos, maps and charts.

Biography in Context

Biographical entries from encyclopedias and journal articles about noteworthy world figures.

Brainfuse

Live homework help and tutoring, including a writing lab, skills building, test prep, and Spanish-speaking tutors, for students of all ages and learning levels.

Britannica

Targeted search levels for children, young adults, and an adult reference center. Current events and primary sources are included.

Britannica Learning Zone

For early learners, a fun activity-centered environment that teaches letters, numbers, words, time, geography and more.

CultureGrams

Illustrated resource featuring cultural and geographical information on provinces, states and world topics.

Daily Life Throughout History

Insider's perspective on daily life in worldwide civilizations, from antiquity to modern day.

EBSCOhost

A collection of database resources with access to comprehensive information and thousands of full-text articles on educational, business, lifestyle and historical topics.

EBSCOhost eBook Collection

A collection of e-books covering a wide range of categories and topics.

Elementary School Explora

Full-text articles for elementary school-age children, including categories organized by popular topics.

Enciclopedia Moderna

Britannica in Spanish for high school and college-level research.

Foundation Center - Access is available only at the Parker location

Databases, directories, fundraising and philanthropic publications, and training resources for finding funding opportunities, scholarships, fellowships and more, and researching philanthropic giving.

Gale Virtual Reference Library

A searchable database by topic or subject, including the arts, literature, business, history and medicine.

General Research Explora

A searchable database that uses various sources, including academic journals, magazines, reference books and more, to provide full-text articles on selected topics.

Global Issues in Context

High School Explora

Literature Resource Center

Lynda.com

Middle School Explora

Full-text articles from popular magazines and reference books, including categories organized by popular topics, for middle schoolers.

Modern World History Online

Facts on File resource of world history, from the mid-15th century to the present.

Newspaper Source

Full-text resource of 45 leading national and international newspapers, as well as 315 regional newspapers.

NoveList

Comprehensive fiction resource with lists, recommendations and series information.

NoveList K-8

Fiction resource for readers of picture books, chapter books, and young adult titles.

Science Reference Center
