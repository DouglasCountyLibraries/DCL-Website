Meeting and Study Room Guidelines

Meeting Room and Castle Rock Community Bandstand Guidelines

  • Programs held in the meeting rooms or bandstand venue must not disrupt the use of the library by others.
  • Please do not attach any material to walls, windows, doors or hallways leading into the meeting rooms or to the bandstand structure.
  • Private parties or receptions are not permitted.
  • Groups are responsible for setup and cleanup of chairs, tables and any refreshments. A fee will be charged for cleaning, repairs or replacements due to excessive damage.
  • Please end meetings 10 minutes before library closure.
  • Light refreshments may be served.
  • The use of alcoholic beverages is prohibited unless special permission is granted by the Library Board of Trustees. Please use the Alcohol Use Request Form

    Alcohol Use Request

    to submit an application for special permission.
  • Use of tobacco is prohibited everywhere in the library.
  • No storage is provided for groups using the library or the bandstand, and the library is not responsible for anything left on the premises.
  • Audiovisual and computer equipment available at each library is listed on the reservation form. All technical support is the responsibility of the group booking the room.
  • The library manager must grant specific permission for any group wanting to charge a fee for supplies, services or fundraising.
  • Permission to use facilities does not constitute endorsement by the library. No advertisement or announcement implying such endorsement is permitted.
  • Nonprofit groups may be asked to provide a copy of their IRS Determination Letter, their Articles of Incorporation, or a Tax Exempt Certificate.
  • Groups may be asked to provide Proof of Insurance.

Study Room Guidelines

  • Rooms may be reserved for a maximum of two hours, once each week and up to one month in advance. Room use may be extended if no one is waiting. Due to large demand for study rooms, your room may be given to another group if you are more than 15 minutes late for your reserved time.
  • All other meeting room guidelines apply.

Castle Rock Community Bandstand

  • Use of the bandstand is free for nonprofit and community groups, which are given preference in reservations. Proof of status may be required in the form of an IRS Form 501(c)(3), Articles of Incorporation, or a Tax Exempt Certificate.
  • For-profit groups may reserve the bandstand when it is available.
  • Groups using the bandstand are limited to one event per month.
  • The bandstand is available for use during the library's regular hours, and before/after hours with the library manager's approval. Restroom facilities will not be available at after-hours events.
  • Events at the bandstand are free and open to the public except by special permission by the library manager; the collection of donations is permissible. CDs or other event-related merchandise may be sold with a valid Business and Sales Tax License from the Town of Castle Rock.
  • Cancellations must be made 24 hours in advance of scheduled usage. Please call (303) 791-7323 in the event of inclement weather.

Meeting Room and Community Bandstand Publicity Guidelines:

  • Library-sponsored meetings, workshops and events are those that are organized, co-organized or specifically requested by library staff. These events are promoted via Douglas County Libraries' standard publicity channels. Please feel free to contact us about promotions for your library-sponsored program.
  • Non-library-sponsored meetings, workshops and events are those scheduled to occur in library meeting rooms or venues, but which do not qualify as library-sponsored events and do not receive support from library staff or resources beyond the provision of the room or venue.
  • Please include the following statement on any/all promotional materials, including but not limited to signs/posters, fliers/mailings, press releases, online promotions, and similar advertising:

[Event] is funded and presented by [organization/individual] and is not sponsored by Douglas County Libraries. For more information, please contact [insert your contact name and phone number; do NOT include library contact information without prior consent from the manager of the library where your meeting will be held].

Customer Service: (303) 791-7323
