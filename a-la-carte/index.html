À La Carte Services

The success of any event often comes down to the details. We aim to provide you and your attendees with the best experience possible by offering an array of à la carte services that you may add to any reservation.

Our À La Carte Services

  • NEW! Informational event posting on DCL’s website: $30 per listing/event
  • Coffee service: $40 per gallon
  • Hot tea service: $20 per gallon
  • Water service (price varies depending on hour of event): $20-30
  • Candy, pens and paper: $4 per attendee
  • Linens (black or white): $10 per linen
  • Sale of goods/services or receipt of donations allowed
  • Pipe and drape décor (black, gold or silver backdrop): $30
