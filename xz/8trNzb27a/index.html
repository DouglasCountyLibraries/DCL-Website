Staff Day 2021

Friday, September 17, Highlands Ranch

Breakout sessions are at 10:45 & 11:30AM

Please register for one presentation or activity per session.

Yoga

All yoga sessions are the same regardless of the title.
Refresh your mind, body and soul with yoga in Civic Green Park! Join expert Yogis as they guide you through stretches, breathing exercises and meditation techniques to calm the mind. Bring a yoga mat or blanket if you have one, although it is not required! Be sure to wear something stretchy and comfortable as there will be lots of movement.

Tai Chi

All Tai Chi sessions are the same regardless of the title.
Refresh your mind, body and soul with tai chi in Civic Green Park! Originally developed for self-defense, tai chi has evolved into a graceful form of exercise used for stress reduction and other health benefits. Often described as meditation in motion, tai chi promotes serenity through gentle, flowing movements. Expert instructors will guide you through beginner movements and techniques. Be sure to wear something stretchy and comfortable as there will be lots of movement.

Renew You, By...You

Read, take a walk, and draw with sidewalk chalk! This session allows you to take some time to renew yourself by taking a walk, enjoying a book outdoors, drawing with sidewalk chalk, or enjoying a make and take on the Highlands Ranch terrace. The make and take is a sea salt scrub to help renew the body and soul.

KonMari

Your home is your sanctuary. Having only the things you need and love will make your home easier to manage and care for. The KonMari method is a most effective way to remove clutter and make your home spark joy! Join Kate Englebrecht, Certified KonMari Master-Level Consultant, in a demonstration of this life-changing method of tidying up. Please bring a piece of clothing that you want to learn how to fold.

The Importance of Mental Health

There has been a growing awareness in our nation recently about the importance of mental health. In this session, we will talk about what "mental health" actually means, what contributes to it, and the landscape of mental health in the U.S. within the context of current events. We will discuss important signs of mental health need, and how to cultivate a daily practice of mental health, connect to services, and help build a company culture that supports it. Phil Stockton, a licensed therapist and Operations Director at Khesed Wellness, a nonprofit that makes mental health services affordable for the underinsured, will present. Phil has worked in mental health since 2009, and is especially passionate about meaning and purpose in life.

Intuitive Eating 2.0: How To Break Free From Dieting & Eat Better Once & For All

Eating well is one of life’s great pleasures, yet more and more people are confused and stressed about food, eating and weight. Diets are restrictive, hard to follow, and simply don’t work. There’s a better way! This workshop introduces the fundamentals of intuitive and competent eating, and helps you learn how to eat to feel your best! Join Suzanne Farrell, a Registered Dietitian Nutritionist, Certified Intuitive Eating Counselor, Master of Nutrition Science and owner of Cherry Creek Nutrition, Inc. With over 20 years of counseling experience, she specializes in a non-diet approach to weight and eating management, disordered eating and fertility nutrition.
