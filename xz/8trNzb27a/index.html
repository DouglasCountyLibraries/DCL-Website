Staff Day 2021

Friday, September 17, Highlands Ranch

Breakout sessions are at 10:30 & 11:30AM

Please register for one presentation or activity per session.
Yoga
Refresh your mind, body and soul with yoga in Civic Green Park! Join expert Yogis as they guide you through stretches, breathing exercises and meditation techniques to calm the mind. Bring a yoga mat or blanket if you have one, although it is not required! Be sure to wear something stretchy and comfortable as there will be lots of movement.
Tai Chi
Refresh your mind, body and soul with tai chi in Civic Green Park! Originally developed for self-defense, tai chi has evolved into a graceful form of exercise used for stress reduction and other health benefits. Often described as meditation in motion, tai chi promotes serenity through gentle, flowing movements. Expert instructors will guide you through beginner movements and techniques. Be sure to wear something stretchy and comfortable as there will be lots of movement.
KonMari
Your home is your sanctuary. Having only the things you need and love will make your home easier to manage and care for. The KonMari method is a most effective way to remove clutter and make your home spark joy! Join Kate Englebrecht, Certified KonMari Master-Level Consultant, in a demonstration of this life-changing method of tidying up. Please bring a piece of clothing that you want to learn how to fold.
