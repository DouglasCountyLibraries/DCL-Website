Breakout Session Descriptions

Bounce Back Stronger: Mastering Resilience

Nhi Aronheim
Location: Kids Corner
When people strengthen their resilience muscle, they become healthier mentally and emotionally, leading to higher work productivity. Resilient people often rise to challenges and do not give up, while nonresilient people often avoid challenges for fear of failure, see criticism rather than constructive feedback, and are reluctant to engage in learning and self-improvement efforts. Hear warm, personal stories about how you can unravel the secrets of resilience and be inspired to turn adversity into your greatest strength!

Building Blocks of CARE

The Science of Resilience for the Workplace

Kaiser Permanente - Glowmundo
Location: Event Hall
This hands-on workshop seeks to build an individual’s capacity to be resilient. Guided by the processes of the Caring Science Theory by the Institute of Heart Math, you will learn about the science of resilience. In addition, you will practice techniques that can be applied through the workday to increase brain function toward job satisfaction and burnout prevention.
