Public Policy Survey

Community Input Needed

Douglas County Libraries' Board of Trustees has worked this year to edit and hone its directives to staff for providing excellent Library services. Thank you for your feedback about DCL policies.
    The Library's Access Policy relies on professional practices that have been in place for nearly a century in North America. They direct the Library to be impartial, allowing citizens, through their own freedom and self-determination, to choose content that is right for them and their family so long as that content is legal.
    What do you want the Library to know about this policy?
    • The Library's Children and Parents Policy encourages parents to make content choices that are right for them. The policy directs the Library to provide parents with consultation and tools for restricting their own children's use of Library resources, without allowing anyone to restrict the choices of someone else's family.
