Printing & Copying

Letter and legal size black and white copies are $.10 per page, or $.20 double-sided. Letter and legal size color copies are $.25 per page, or $.50 double-sided. 11” X 17” black and white copies are $.20 per page, or $.40 double-sided. 11” X 17” color copies are $.50 per page, or $1.00 double-sided. Mastercard and Visa accepted with a $1.00 minimum charge per transaction.

Faxing

The library sends faxes from the Highlands Ranch, Lone Tree, Parker, Castle Rock and Roxborough locations. The fee is $1.75 for the first page and $1.00 for each additional page. The library cannot accept incoming faxes and cannot send international faxes.

Digital Collections

Hoopla

Instantly stream digital movies, TV, music, e-books and audiobooks. There’s no waiting, and items are returned automatically (so no late fees either)!

Zinio Magazines

Download digital magazines to read on your computer, tablet or mobile device.

Freegal Music

Download up to five songs a week for free using your library card number and PIN. This service offers access to the Sony Music Entertainment catalog — no special software needed.

Overdrive

Access a collection of e-books and audiobooks to enjoy on your PC, smartphone or e-book reader.

Homebound Delivery

Qualified card holders can have library materials delivered right to their doors if they are unable to come to the library due to illness or disability.
