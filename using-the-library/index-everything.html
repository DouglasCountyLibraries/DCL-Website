Printing & Copying

Letter and legal size black and white copies are $.10 per page, or $.20 double-sided. Letter and legal size color copies are $.25 per page, or $.50 double-sided. 11” X 17” black and white copies are $.20 per page, or $.40 double-sided. 11” X 17” color copies are $.50 per page, or $1.00 double-sided. Mastercard and Visa accepted with a $1.00 minimum charge per transaction.

Faxing

The library sends faxes from the Highlands Ranch, Lone Tree, Parker, Castle Rock and Roxborough locations. The fee is $1.75 for the first page and $1.00 for each additional page. The library cannot accept incoming faxes and cannot send international faxes.

Proctoring

We are pleased to offer free test proctoring at three locations, by appointment. Some restrictions apply.

Proctoring Guidelines

  • Before a proctoring appointment may be made, we must have received written confirmation from your school that they accept our guidelines and/or we must have approved your school's proctoring guidelines.
  • Proctors are unable to stay in the room with you when you test. We cannot store valuables for you.
  • Unless you specified that your exam must be proctored by someone with a Master of Library Science degree, we may provide a proctor without an MLS.
  • Please make sure your exam reaches your assigned proctor at least 48 hours before your proctoring appointment.
  • You must present a current photo ID before each exam. Minors who do not have a photo ID must be accompanied by a parent with a photo ID.
  • Please be advised that requests from the school for a staff member's personal identification will be declined. Examples of personal identification numbers that will not be provided include Social Security Number (even if the request is only for the last four digits), Driver's License Number, Employee Number.
  • We can scan and email completed exams to your school, free of charge. You are responsible for any costs, including postage and an envelope, for returning the completed exam by mail.
