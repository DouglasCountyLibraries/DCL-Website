Event & Hospitality Services

Your event is important to us! Our packages accommodate the style, size and complexity of any event, ranging from Self-Service to Concierge Service, where a dedicated event expert helps you make your special function a success.
Have questions? Call Talayna Akrie, Event Planner Department Head, at (303) 688-7605.

Self-Service

Overview
If you want to host a simple meeting, this basic service puts you fully in control. There is no room charge, and all aspects of the event are your responsibility. Drop-off catering is permitted. If you want to reserve 50-100% of the library, serve alcohol, or use a full-service caterer with banquet staff, upgrade to Concierge Service. Choose Select Service or Concierge Service if you want to sell tickets or charge an entry fee.
Details
You:
  • Reserve your space.
  • Coordinate vendors and payments.
  • Set up and return the room to its pre-event state.
  • Maintain the room during your event.
  • Manage your event agenda.
  • Handle any financial aspects of your event.
Reserve a Meeting Room

Select Service

Overview
This package blends the benefits of Self-Service with expert consultation from a DCL event specialist, who will take on some planning and hosting responsibilities to ensure the success of your event. Drop-off catering is allowed with this service, and supplemental furniture may be rented through DCL’s preferred vendor. Select Service must be booked at least 48 hours in advance. If you want to reserve 50-100% of the library, serve alcohol, or use a full-service caterer with banquet staff, upgrade to Concierge Service. Choose Select Service or Concierge Service if you want to sell tickets or charge an entry fee.
Details
All aspects of Self-Service apply, except that we will set up and return the room to its pre-event state for you.
A DCL event specialist will also:
  • Consult with you prior to your event to determine your needs and special requests.
  • Communicate with you by phone or email prior to your event to keep things on track.
  • Provide recommendations for catering and other vendors based on the type and style of your event.

Concierge Service

Overview
This full-service package provides a premium event experience — from initial consultation through event completion. Our expert event planners take the reins to tie together all aspects of your special event. Full-service and drop-off catering are allowed with this service. You may also rent supplemental furniture through the library’s preferred vendor. Concierge Service may be reserved up to three years in advance, and must be booked at least six weeks in advance.
Details

You receive the benefits of Select Service, plus we will:

  • Make your reservation for you (including priority booking for eligible groups).
  • Maintain the room during your event.
  • Manage your event agenda.
A DCL event specialist will also help you:
  • Negotiate with vendors regarding timing, pricing and contracts.
  • Track your expenses and maintain your established budget.

Customer Service: (303) 791-7323
