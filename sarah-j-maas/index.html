DCL Presents:

Sarah J. Maas

Sarah J. Maas is the #1 New York Times bestselling author of the Throne of Glass series. Ticket includes a reserved seat at the author's presentation, a signed hardcover copy of Tower of Dawn, exclusive swag, and a chance to be one of 50 attendees to meet Sarah and have a book personalized. Book sales will be provided courtesy of Tattered Cover Book Store.

Lone Tree Arts Center

Wednesday, September 13, 2017 | 6:30PM

Tickets $25

Buy Now!

Plus, be sure to enter our Instagram contest for a chance to win:

  • A free event ticket, including a signed hardcover book, and a guaranteed meeting/photo opportunity with Sarah J. Maas
  • Your winning Instagram photo printed, mounted for hanging, and signed by Sarah J. Maas

How to enter:

  • Follow @dclcolorado on Instagram
  • Share a photo on your own feed of a #bookstagram featuring one of Sarah J. Maas's books
  • Tag @dclcolorado in the post and use the hashtag, #SJMatDCL
One winner will be selected by Douglas County Libraries staff. We will be looking for dynamic, creative photos. The contest closes on Monday, August 28, at 6PM MDT.

#SJMatDCL Instagram Contest Rules

  • No purchase necessary. Purchase of event ticket does not enhance chance of winning; if an event ticket is purchased, the winner will be refunded the price of the purchased ticket. This contest is hosted by Douglas County Libraries (DCL). This contest is open only to Colorado residents. Contest closes at 6PM (MDT), August 28, 2017. The winner of the best picture will be chosen and notified by September 1, 2017.
  • Judging will be based on the aesthetic quality of the photo and the best incorporation of a Sarah J. Maas book.
  • DCL will share the winning photo and the name of the winner on DCL social media channels and the website.
  • ONE winner will receive: a free event ticket, including a signed hardcover book, and a guaranteed meeting/photo opportunity with Sarah J. Maas, as well as the printed winning Instagram photo, mounted for hanging and signed by Sarah J. Maas.
  • This contest is not sponsored or endorsed by Instagram. By entering the contest, you agree to these rules. Void where prohibited.
