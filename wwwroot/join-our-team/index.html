Welcome to Douglas County Libraries, where exciting things happen every day at our seven library locations. When you join our team, you become part of a dynamic network of doers. Differing talents and diverse backgrounds are valued. Yes, we work hard, but we also infuse fun (and integrity and excellence) into everything we do. And what we do is engage, educate and entertain Douglas County residents to transform lives and build community. You, too, can contribute to this narrative. Join us!

Our Story

Douglas County Libraries is a passionate advocate for literacy and lifelong learning. It’s a place where purpose and profession align and you’re empowered to make a positive difference. Our five core values are the heart of our organization’s culture. They guide the work we do, and also the way we interact with each other and our patrons. We’re readers and leaders who work together in all manner of creative, impactful ways to meet goals and fulfill our mission. It’s a great story to be part of!

A Collection of Perks

Work-life Balance
We love our work, and we work hard. But we also have generous paid time off and floating holidays, so it’s possible to enjoy everything else in life too.
Compensation and Security
Earning now while saving for later makes for a happy ending when you close the book on your career. Douglas County Libraries offers competitive salaries, a pension plan through Colorado Public Employees’ Retirement Association (PERA), and an optional 401(k).
Health and Well-being
Our comprehensive, competitive benefits package includes health, dental, vision, disability and life insurance; extended illness pay; and an employee assistance program.
Growth and Development
There’s room for professional growth and promotion through organizational development, training programs, and tuition assistance. Come grow with us!
