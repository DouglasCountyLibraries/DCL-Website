BizInfo: Build Your Business Here

Douglas County Libraries' information service tailored especially for entrepreneurs and small businesses.

Use our BizInfo form:

Provide us with information about your business and what type of research help you would like and a business librarian will respond to your inquiry in one business day.

For Entrepreneurs:

We can help you find example business plans, licensing procedures, demographic statistics and a variety of other information for your start-up.

For established businesses

We can provide assistance with market research and lists, competitive intelligence, industry trends and much more.

Ask Us Your Small Business Question

Library Services for Small Business

Tools for Business

Tools for Business is just one of the many resources Douglas County Libraries makes available to small business that features local, state, and federal resources to start and grow businesses. Tools for Business contains a business start-up kit with all the forms to comply with government requirements, links to funding resources, green business practices, business tax incentives, employer resources and more.

Technology

All Douglas County Libraries have free public computing stations equipped with high-speed Internet access, as well as productivity software. Free wireless Internet access and mobile printing is also available to all guests.

Meeting & Study Rooms

Most library branches have at least one large meeting room available for public use. In addition, the libraries in Castle Rock, Highlands Ranch and Parker have small study rooms that can be reserved on short notice. These smaller rooms are perfect for meeting with clients and advisers. Visit the library location of choice for more details and reservations or call (303) 791-7323.

Special Services

Three libraries have special services and programs developed especially for its surrounding community.
Philip S. Miller - Castle Rock: Research instruction by reference librarians on-site at your business.
Parker Library: Small business workshops and seminars. Computer software classes.
James H. LaRue - Highlands Ranch: Community Business Directory at the Reference Desk.
